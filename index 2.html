Hussein Chalayan

Exhibition Walkthrough · Somerset House · London Fashion Week

Exhibition Overview

This immersive exhibition traces Hussein Chalayan’s pioneering exploration of movement, transformation, and the space between body, garment, and technology. Visitors journey through a sequence of environments that shift from stillness to motion and back again.

Walkthrough Journey

1 — Entrance: Curiosity & Stillness

A calm, minimal space where garments appear motionless. Subtle light shifts and soft ambient sound respond gently to visitor movement, building anticipation.

2 — Kinetic Displays: Digital Transformations

Projection mapping, light, and motion sensors create the illusion of garments transforming from fabric into light, with subtle mechanical and fabric-like sounds enhancing the effect.

3 — Moving Still Rooms: Frozen Runway Moments

Archival Chalayan runway pieces are shown alongside slow-motion projections of their original transformations, allowing visitors to study movement that has been visually suspended in time.

4 — Interactive Performance Zone: Becoming Part of the Work

Visitors activate transformations themselves through AR mirrors, responsive projections, and motion-reactive visuals that place their own bodies into the performance.

5 — Reflective Space: Return to Stillness

A quiet, low-sensory area with minimal sound and soft lighting where visitors can pause, reflect, and absorb the themes of movement, transformation, and stillness.

6 — Tactile Workshop Area: Material Exploration

Hands-on tables allow visitors to handle fabric samples and mechanical components, exploring the materials and construction methods behind Chalayan’s transformative designs.
