Hussein Chalayan

Exhibition Walkthrough · Somerset House · London Fashion Week

Welcome

This exhibition explores Hussein Chalayan’s pioneering fusion of fashion, technology, migration, and identity. Follow the walkthrough to experience key works in the order curated for London Fashion Week at Somerset House.

Exhibition Stops

1. Temporal Architecture

Chalayan’s iconic transformable garments challenge the boundaries between clothing and environment. These pieces examine displacement and portable identity.

2. Technology & Movement

A showcase of mechanised and remote-controlled designs where garments morph in real time, redefining the relationship between body and machine.

3. Memory & Migration

Film and fashion intersect in works exploring themes of cultural transition, borders, and emotional geography.

4. Future Materials

Experimental textiles and sculptural silhouettes demonstrate Chalayan’s role as a visionary shaping the future of design.
